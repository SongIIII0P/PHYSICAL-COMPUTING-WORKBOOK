Week07

Hey, Arduino starter kit…
What are you?
Every single piece looks like it’s from another planet.
The only thing I recognise is the wire that I saw
in a dusty old science class back in primary school.

It’s not even just a wire — it’s called a Jumper wire.
Like… what are you even?
Are you clothing? Are you static electricity?
What are you?

Circuit…
you mysterious thing.
Why are you so complicated?
I always thought you were just that circular line
on the school sports field.

Turns out you’re an electric circuit?


You’re… surprisingly interesting.

And you’re not even alone, are you?
You’re always together with that jumper wire and resistor.

Oh, and the funny part?
I thought “resistor” was “register.”
Ha… Week one of physical computing,
you’ve already brought me
nothing but confusion and quiet sorrow.

But I have to admit.
It’s kind of fun.
The push-button things?

When you press that little button,
the light turns on.

And...
the fan starts spinning.
It’s… honestly kind of amazing.
